## General

### Who is behind Aidxn Design?
Aidxn Design is a solo full-stack web design agency run by Aiden Wood, based in Queensland, Australia. You work directly with the person building your project — no handoffs, no miscommunication.

### What types of projects do you take on?
We specialise in custom websites, branding, and digital marketing for small-to-medium businesses. This includes company websites, landing pages, e-commerce stores, and web applications.

### How long does a typical project take?
Timelines vary by scope:

| Project Type | Typical Timeline |
|---|---|
| Landing page | 1-2 weeks |
| Business website (5-10 pages) | 3-5 weeks |
| E-commerce site | 6-10 weeks |
| Web application | 8-16 weeks |
| Branding package | 2-4 weeks |

### Do you work with clients outside Australia?
Yes. Most communication happens asynchronously via email and shared project tools, so timezone differences are manageable.

## Technical

### What technology do you use?
Our core stack includes Astro, React, Tailwind CSS, and Netlify. For more detail, see our [tech stack documentation](/docs/tech-stack/).

### Will my site be fast?
Performance is a core priority. Velocity-powered sites consistently achieve 90+ Lighthouse scores across performance, accessibility, SEO, and best practices.

### Can I update content myself?
Yes. We can integrate a headless CMS (content management system) so you can update text, images, and pages without needing to write code.

### Do you provide hosting?
Sites are deployed to Netlify, which provides global CDN hosting, automatic SSL certificates, and continuous deployment. Hosting costs are typically covered in your maintenance plan.

## Working Together

### How do we communicate during a project?
Primarily via email, with scheduled video calls for kickoff, design review, and launch. You will also have access to a shared project space for reviewing deliverables.

### What if I need changes after launch?
We offer post-launch support windows (30, 90, or ongoing depending on your package). After that, changes can be handled on a per-request basis or through a monthly retainer.

### What is your payment structure?
Typically:
- **50% upfront** — Paid before work begins
- **50% on completion** — Paid before the site goes live

For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments.

### Do you offer ongoing support?
Yes. Monthly retainer packages are available for ongoing updates, maintenance, analytics reporting, and ad management.
