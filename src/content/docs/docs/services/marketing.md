## Overview

We help businesses grow with data-driven digital marketing. From Google Ads campaigns to analytics dashboards, every dollar is tracked and optimised.

## Services

### Google Ads Management
Full-service campaign management including:

- **Search campaigns** — Targeting high-intent keywords for your services
- **Display campaigns** — Brand awareness across the Google Display Network
- **Remarketing** — Re-engaging visitors who did not convert
- **Conversion tracking** — Ensuring every lead and sale is measured
- **Monthly reporting** — Clear reports showing spend, results, and ROI

### SEO
On-page and technical SEO to improve your organic search visibility:

- **Technical audit** — Site speed, crawlability, and indexation
- **On-page optimisation** — Meta tags, headings, content structure
- **Local SEO** — Google Business Profile optimisation for local searches
- **Content strategy** — Keyword research and content planning

### Analytics & Dashboards
Custom analytics solutions using:

- **Google Analytics 4** — Event tracking and conversion measurement
- **Google BigQuery** — Large-scale data analysis and custom reporting
- **Custom dashboards** — Real-time performance visibility for your team

## Reporting

Every client receives regular performance reports covering:

- Campaign spend and budget utilisation
- Key metrics (impressions, clicks, conversions, cost per conversion)
- Trend analysis and period-over-period comparisons
- Actionable recommendations for the next period
