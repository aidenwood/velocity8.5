## Overview

A strong brand is the foundation of every great digital presence. We create cohesive visual identities that communicate who you are and resonate with your audience.

## Services

### Logo Design
Custom logo design with multiple concepts, revisions, and final delivery in all formats you need (SVG, PNG, favicon, social media variants).

### Brand Guidelines
A comprehensive brand guide covering:

- **Colour palette** — Primary, secondary, and accent colours with hex/RGB values
- **Typography** — Font selections, sizing scales, and usage rules
- **Logo usage** — Clear space, minimum sizes, and placement guidelines
- **Tone of voice** — How your brand communicates in writing
- **Imagery style** — Photography and illustration direction

### Visual Identity System
Beyond the logo — we design a complete visual system including:

- Business card and stationery design
- Social media templates and profile assets
- Email signature design
- Presentation templates
- Iconography and illustration style

## Our Approach

1. **Research** — Understanding your market, competitors, and target audience
2. **Exploration** — Multiple concept directions based on research insights
3. **Refinement** — Iterating on the chosen direction until it is perfect
4. **Delivery** — Final files, brand guidelines, and all assets packaged for use

## Tools We Use

- **Figma** — Primary design tool for all brand work
- **Affinity Designer** — Vector illustration and print-ready exports
- **After Effects** — Animated logo and motion brand assets
