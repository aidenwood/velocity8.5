Every project at Aidxn Design follows a clear four-phase process. This keeps things moving, sets expectations early, and ensures nothing falls through the cracks.

## 1. Discovery

We start by understanding your business, your audience, and your goals. This phase includes:

- **Kickoff call** — A focused conversation about what you need and why
- **Competitive analysis** — Understanding your market and what your competitors are doing
- **Requirements gathering** — Defining scope, features, and success metrics
- **Timeline and budget alignment** — Making sure we are on the same page before any work begins

**Deliverable:** A project brief outlining scope, timeline, and key milestones.

## 2. Design

With a clear brief in hand, we move into visual design:

- **Wireframes** — Low-fidelity layouts to nail down structure and flow
- **Brand alignment** — Ensuring the design reflects your identity (or building one if needed)
- **High-fidelity mockups** — Polished designs in Figma for review and feedback
- **Iteration** — Refining based on your feedback until we get it right

**Deliverable:** Approved Figma designs ready for development.

## 3. Develop

This is where the design becomes a real, working product:

- **Component-driven development** — Building reusable, maintainable code with Astro and React
- **Performance optimisation** — Ensuring fast load times and strong Lighthouse scores
- **Responsive implementation** — Every breakpoint tested and polished
- **CMS integration** — Content management setup where applicable
- **Testing** — Cross-browser and device testing

**Deliverable:** A fully functional staging site for review.

## 4. Deploy

Once everything is approved, we go live:

- **Deployment to Netlify** — Production-grade hosting with global CDN
- **DNS and domain setup** — Pointing your domain to the new site
- **Analytics configuration** — Google Analytics and/or custom dashboards
- **Handover documentation** — Everything you need to manage your site
- **Post-launch support** — A support window to catch any issues after launch

**Deliverable:** Your live website, fully deployed and monitored.
